# News Collection - 2026-06-19

## Vaca Muerta & Energy
### Chevron's El Trapial RIGI Project
- Source: EconoJournal
- Chevron Argentina submitted a RIGI application for USD 13.8 billion for the development of El Trapial. This is one of the largest individual upstream investments presented to date.

### Southern Energy GNL & Adani Group
- Source: Minergy / MDZ
- Indian conglomerate Adani Group acquired 51% of Meridian Transportes Marítimos.
- Meridian will operate the port services at Punta Colorada (Río Negro) for the Southern Energy GNL project (PAE, YPF, Pampa Energía, Golar LNG).
- Adani secured a 10-year contract for these services.

### Vaca Muerta Sur (VMOS) Pipeline
- Source: La Nueva / Various
- Consists of a 437 km pipeline from Allen to Punta Colorada.
- Investment: USD 2,486 million.
- Partners: YPF, PAE, Vista, Pampa Energía, Pluspetrol, Chevron, Shell.
- Target operation: Late 2026.

### Production Records (April 2026)
- Source: INDEC / MASE
- IPI Minero up 9.5% interannually.
- Crude oil production: 4,215k m3 (+19.1% YoY).
- Non-conventional oil (Vaca Muerta): 2,915k m3 (+39.2% YoY).
- Gas: 4,218M m3 (+2.8% YoY), non-conventional gas up 12.2%.

## Mining
### Copper: San Jorge (Mendoza) RIGI Approval
- Source: Diario Río Negro / MDZ
- Resolution 801/2026 formalizes the entry of the San Jorge project (Uspallata, Mendoza) into the RIGI.
- Project led by Zonda Metals GmbH and Alberdi Energy.
- Committed investment around USD 891 million.

### Copper: Distrito Vicuña RIGI Approval
- Source: La Nación
- Formal approval for the USD 9,700 million investment in the Vicuña cluster (Josemaría/Filo del Sol).

### Lithium: Ganfeng & Lithium Argentina
- Source: La Nación
- Ganfeng and Lithium Argentina are actively seeking a third strategic partner/investor for their lithium operations.

### Regional: Río Negro Mining
- Source: Diario Neuquino
- Rio Negro starts production at Calcatreu (gold/silver, Patagonia Gold).
- Province is looking to expand into Uranium, Copper, and Lithium.
