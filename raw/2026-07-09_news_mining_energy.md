# News Mining & Energy Argentina - 2026-07-09

## Mining & Energy Macro Stats
- **Mining Growth (May 2026):** The sector grew 9.5% YoY, driven by Vaca Muerta and the lithium boom (INDEC). CAEM projects exports to reach US$ 9,000M in 2026 (+49% YoY).
- **RIGI Status (July 8, 2026):**
    - Total projects presented: 39 initiatives worth US$ 121,237 million.
    - Approved: 15/16 projects (approx. US$ 20,247 million).
    - Rejected: 1 (Mariana lithium - Ganfeng, due to 'new investment' rule).
    - Pending: 23 projects in evaluation.
- **Mining Electricity Demand (OLACDE Report):** Projections indicate that demand will quintuple by 2034, driven by copper and lithium projects in the NOA region.

## Oil & Gas (Vaca Muerta)
- **Production Records (May 2026):** Peak of 903,700 bpd oil (19.6% YoY growth), 69% shale contribution. Gas reached 115.14 million m3/d.
- **RIGI Approvals:**
    - **Pampa Energía:** Rincón de Aranda project approved for US$ 4,521 million.
    - **Chevron:** El Trapial unconventional development approved for US$ 13,800 million.
- **New Investments:** Central Puerto advances in Vaca Muerta with US$ 600 million for its first wells.
- **Vaca Muerta Sur (VMOS):** The USD 2.5 billion pipeline project is over 59% complete. Milestone: installation of six 120,000 m³ tanks at Punta Colorada terminal.
- **Southern Energy GNL:** Total RIGI investment confirmed at US$ 8,178 billion (PAE/Golar barge + Gasoducto San Matías).

## Mining Projects & Regional Updates
- **RIGI Approvals (July 2026):**
    - **Minera San Jorge (Mendoza, Copper):** US$ 891 million investment approved. Focus on Uspallata.
    - **Cauchari-Olaroz Expansion (Jujuy, Lithium):** Ganfeng/Lithium Argentina/JEMSE project approved for US$ 1,241 million (targeting 40k tpa).
- **Lithium:** Production increased 47.5% in the first five months of 2026 (INDEC).
- **Mendoza:**
    - **Don Luis Project:** Lithium project received legislative approval under Law 7.722.
    - **Chile-Argentina Treaty:** Reactivation of the binational mining treaty to facilitate logistics in San Juan and Mendoza clusters.
- **BESS (Energy Storage):** AlmaGBA Tender awarded 700 MW of Battery Energy Storage Systems to reinforce the SADI grid and mitigate peak demand.
