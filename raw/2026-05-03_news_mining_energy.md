# News Feed: Mining & Energy Argentina - 2026-05-03

## Vaca Muerta & Energy
- **Vista Energy Strategic Push:** Miguel Galuccio announced that Vista will present RIGI applications for the Águila Mora and Bandurria Norte blocks by the end of Q2 2026. The goal is to accelerate developments originally planned for 2030. 2026 production target raised to 143,000 boe/d; lifting cost reported at US$ 4.3/bbl. (Source: EconoJournal, Latin Energy Group)
- **Pluspetrol Megaproject:** Requested RIGI entry for a US$ 12,000 million investment in Bajo del Choique–La Invernada (Neuquén), aiming for 600 wells and 100,000 bpd. (Source: Memory/Industry Reports)
- **Southern Energy GNL:** Pan American Energy and Golar LNG confirmed a US$ 6.9B investment for a floating GNL production unit in the San Matías Gulf, Río Negro. (Source: Argenports, Industry reports May 2)
- **Pampa Energía Fertilizer Plant:** RIGI application for a US$ 2,400M urea plant in Bahía Blanca using Vaca Muerta gas. (Source: Memory)
- **Vaca Muerta Sur (VMOS):** Pipeline reached 58% completion; target for exports is December 2026. (Source: LM Neuquén/Mase)
- **YPF Frontier Expansion:** Push into D-129 (Chubut) and acceleration of Palermo Aike (Santa Cruz) to replicate the shale model. (Source: Industry reports May 2)

## Copper
- **Lunahuasi (Distrito Vicuña):** Record copper grades confirmed (peak 18.9% Cu). Dubbed the "Vaca Muerta of Copper". (Source: Mining Industry Status May 2026)
- **Infrastructure Conflict (San Juan):** ENRE Resolution 219/2026 highlights the clash between McEwen Copper (Los Azules) and BHP/Lundin (Distrito Vicuña) over priority access to the 500kV power line. (Source: Boletín Oficial, Tiempo de San Juan)
- **Taca Taca Ratification:** First Quantum ratified USD 5,250M investment under IFC ESG standards. Urgency for EIA and water concessions to meet July RIGI deadline. (Source: Memory, Panorama Minero)
- **Mendoza Reactivation:** Province officially joined the 'Mesa del Cobre' and is pushing a copper-focused strategy. (Source: Memory)

## Lithium
- **Catamarca Judicial Milestone:** Court of Justice lifted the injunction on the Río Los Patos basin (May 2, 2026), enabling expansion for Arcadium and Posco. (Source: Judicial Reports)
- **Posco Argentina:** Integrated HMN block (US$ 65M acquisition). Facing 18-month wait for RIGI approval. (Source: Industry reports April-May)
- **Rio Tinto Rincón:** Secured US$ 1.175B from IFC/BID Invest for its US$ 2.5B project. First 200-ton shipment to Shanghai completed in April. (Source: Memory, May 2 reports)
- **Galan Lithium (HMW):** Construction reached 92% as of May 1; processing start imminent. (Source: Discovery Alert)
- **Lithium Regional Committee:** Formally created in Jujuy. Carlos Sadir (Jujuy) as president; Fernanda Ávila as Secretary. (Source: Memory April 30)

## RIGI & Frameworks
- **BCRA Financial Impact:** Vladimir Werning confirmed RIGI projects liquidated USD 1,205M gross and USD 762M net into reserves. (Source: BCRA / Diario Río Negro)
- **Resolution 484/2026:** Modified profitability ratio requirement from 30% to 35% for RIGI to accommodate shale oil decline curves. (Source: Boletín Oficial)
- **Total Investment:** RIGI applications exceed US$ 52,000M, with 98% in Mining/Energy. (Source: Bloomberg Línea)
- **RIMI:** New regime for investments under US$ 200M enacted. (Source: Memory)

## Logistics & Infrastructure
- **Corredor Bioceánico:** Porto Murtinho-Carmelo Peralta bridge at 82.5% progress. (Source: Memory May 2)
- **Bioceanic Gas Pipeline:** Operational conditions defined between Argentina and Paraguay for Vaca Muerta gas exports. (Source: Memory April 27)
- **Salta Infrastructure:** Acceleration of RN51 to support 18 mining projects. (Source: Memory)
