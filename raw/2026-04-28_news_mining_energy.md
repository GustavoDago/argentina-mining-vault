# News Roundup - 2026-04-28

## RIGI (Regime for Large Investments)
- **BCRA Impact:** The Central Bank confirmed that projects under RIGI have liquidated USD 1,205 million gross and USD 762 million net into reserves as of late April 2024.
- **Investment Volume:** Applications have exceeded USD 52,000 million, with 98% concentrated in Mining and Energy.
- **Regulatory Updates:**
  - Decree 105/2026 extends RIGI benefits to the entire oil & gas upstream segment.
  - Resolution 484/2026 adjusts the profitability ratio requirement from 30% to 35% for projects with steep decline curves (shale oil).
  - Resolution 431/2026: Formal approval for Minera del Altiplano S.A.
  - Resolution 413/2026: Approval for Veladero expansion (Phase 8/9).
- **New Entries:** Pampa Energía requested entry for a fertilizer plant in Bahía Blanca (USD 2,400 million). Gualcamayo 'Carbonatos Profundos' (USD 665 million) was approved under RIGI.

## Energy & Vaca Muerta
- **Operational Record:** Vaca Muerta reached a record 1,700 fracture stages in April 2026.
- **Strategic Alliances:** YPF and ENI (Italy) signed a strategic agreement for LNG exports.
- **Infrastructure:** Vaca Muerta Sur pipeline reaches 58% completion; targeted export operations by December 2026.
- **Corporate Moves:** Vista Energy is assessing RIGI eligibility for Bandurria Norte, Águila Mora, and Bajo del Toro.
- **Exploration:** YPF accelerating Palermo Aike (Santa Cruz) and starting exploration in D-129 formation (Chubut).
- **Gas Export:** Paraguay and Argentina defined operational conditions for the Bioceanic Gas Pipeline to export Vaca Muerta gas to the Capricorn region.
- **ENARGAS:** Resolution 436/2026 simplifies GNV registration and maintenance.

## Mining - Copper
- **Taca Taca (Salta):** Ratified USD 5,250 million investment; agreement with IFC for sustainability standards. Target production: 250k-291k tons/year.
- **Lunahuasi (San Juan):** Exceptional results confirmed (4.08% Cu over 300m, peak 18.9%). Dubbed the 'Vaca Muerta of Copper'.
- **Infrastructure Conflict:** McEwen Copper (Los Azules) filed formal opposition against BHP/Lundin regarding priority for the 500kV power line in San Juan (Resolution 219/2026).
- **Distrito Vicuña:** BHP-Lundin district projects USD 18,000 million total investment. Josemaría Phase 1 construction targeted for Q1 2027.
- **Piuquenes:** Discovery of new copper porphyry system by Andina Copper (USD 27.5M exploration commitment).
- **Mendoza:** Joined 'Mesa del Cobre'; launching copper-focused strategy.

## Mining - Lithium
- **Export Leadership:** Argentina surpassed Chile as the world's second-largest lithium exporter.
- **Rio Tinto Rincón:** First commercial shipment (200 tons) to Shanghai on April 10, 2026. Secured USD 1,175 million financing from IFC/IDB.
- **Posco:** Integration of Hombre Muerto Norte (HMN) to optimize Sal de Oro infrastructure.
- **Catamarca:** Judicial ruling ratified lifting of Río Los Patos injunction, enabling expansions for Arcadium and Posco.
- **Jujuy:** Assumed presidency of Mesa del Litio; implemented 'mini RIGI' for investments starting at USD 5 million.

## Logistics & Regional
- **Corredor Bioceánico:** Porto Murtinho-Carmelo Peralta bridge reached 82.5% progress. Brazil ratified TIR Convention.
- **Mining Diplomacy:** Presidents of Chile and Argentina proposed strategic integration of mining logistics.
