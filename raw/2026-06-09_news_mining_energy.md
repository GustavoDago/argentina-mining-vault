# News Roundup - 2026-06-09

## Energy & Vaca Muerta
- **TGS Gasoducto Perito Moreno Expansion**: The government approved a RIGI project for >$500M (confirmed $550M in some reports) to expand the Perito Moreno pipeline. The project, led by TGS, will add 12 million m3/day of transport capacity by winter 2027. (Source: parlamentario.com, 2026-05-04; updated June 2026)
- **TGS & YPF NGL Project**: YPF has joined TGS in a massive project to process and transport Natural Gas Liquids (NGLs) from Vaca Muerta to Bahía Blanca. This includes a new poliducto and fractionation facilities. (Source: Infobae, 2026-06-07)
- **Fuel Tax Postponement**: The national government postponed the fuel tax increase to July 1, 2026, via Decreto 405/2026. (Source: BORA, June 2026)

## Mining & Copper
- **Taca Taca (Copper)**: First Quantum Minerals updated the investment requirement for the Taca Taca project in Salta to **US$ 5,250 million**. The company is finalizing its RIGI application and environmental impact studies. (Source: dataportuaria.ar, 2026-06-09)
- **Production Boom**: Mining activity grew 9.5% interannually in April 2026. Lithium production saw a record 79% jump. (Source: noticiasdemineria.com.ar, 2026-06-09)
- **Export Projections**: Mining is projected to generate 1 out of every 10 export dollars for Argentina in 2026, surpassing US$ 9,000 million. (Source: ambito.com, 2026-06-07)

## RIGI Framework
- **Investment Concentration**: RIGI presentations have reached **US$ 133,000 million**. Neuquén concentrates 47% (US$ 62,434M) due to Vaca Muerta, while San Juan accounts for US$ 25,000M (mainly copper). Energy represents 64% of the total volume. (Source: alsurnoticias.com.ar, 2026-06-08)
- **TGS Approval**: Transportadora de Gas del Sur received formal RIGI approval for its Gasoducto Perito Moreno expansion (US$ 550M). (Source: 2026-06-08)

## Logistics & Corredor Bioceánico
- **CEPAL Study**: A new study presented in Salta highlights that the Corredor Bioceánico Capricornio is already an operational reality. Lithium exports from NOA are actively utilizing Paso de Jama to reach Asian markets via Chilean ports, reducing transit times by up to 10 days. (Source: globalports.com.ar, 2026-05-30; updated June 2026)
- **Eramine Logistics**: Eramine successfully exported lithium via the Corridor, confirming its effectiveness for the Puna region. (Source: June 2026)
