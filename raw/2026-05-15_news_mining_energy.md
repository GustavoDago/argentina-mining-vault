# News Mining & Energy Argentina - 2026-05-15

## RIGI Approvals & Updates
- **San Jorge (Mendoza):** Formal approval for entry into RIGI. Investment: USD 891 million. Spearheaded by Zonda Metals GmbH and Alberdi Energy. Aim: Copper and Gold production in Uspallata. First major metal mining RIGI in Mendoza.
- **Cauchari-Olaroz Expansion (Jujuy):** Approved for RIGI. Investment: USD 1,241 million. Partners: Ganfeng Lithium, America Lithium, and JEMSE. Target: 40,000 tonnes/year of lithium carbonate.
- **TGS (Gasoducto Perito Moreno):** Resolution 676/2026 (BORA 13/05/2026). Approved for RIGI. Investment: USD 550 million. Adds 14 MMm3/day capacity. Savings of USD 700M/year in imports.
- **Status update:** Total of 16 projects approved under RIGI as of mid-May 2026. Total investment pipeline exceeds USD 95,000 million.
- **Regulatory Change:** Resolution 484/2026 modified the profitability ratio requirement for RIGI from 30% to 35% to adapt to shale oil decline curves.

## Copper
- **Taca Taca (Salta):** First Quantum Minerals published updated NI 43-101 technical report. Investment increased to USD 5,250 million. Projected production: 291,000 tonnes/year of copper. Preparing formal RIGI application.
- **Los Azules (San Juan):** Previously approved under RIGI (USD 2,672 million). Green copper focus.

## Lithium
